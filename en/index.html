    software with purpose

    that unites our energy with your ideas

    Nos espelhamos no ofício carinhoso dos artesãos para colocarmos a alma em cada bit produzido. Abraçamos as tecnologias, práticas e metodologias do século 21 para alavancar a construção de software. A intenção é utilizar o trabalho desempenhado como ferramenta de mudança social, sustentabilidade e resolução de problemas pertinentes em nossas vidas.

    • Excellent quality software design and production

    • Growing and steadily value delivery

    • Realization of truly productive partnerships

    Services

    The world of technology offers a huge range of options and we need to be versatile to meet your needs.
    This is where we place our personality. Our minds do not stop.

    Development

    We produce software from requirements gathering to deployment. We research, prototype, increment and humanize all our work in favor of the solutions we build. Constant dialogue and response to change are key points.

    Consulting

    We absorb the misses and hits on every project. The goal is to detect inconsistencies in the application of methodologies and processes to solidify the development stages and analysis aiming a promising life cycle of software engineering.

    Training

    We are unselfish. We conduct training of tools, techniques and concepts mastered in order to develop the expertise symbiotically. Willpower to share wisdom is the reason for us to learn more and more.
